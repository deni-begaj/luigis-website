If you are looking for a delicious, healthy, amazing italian food cuisine with great prices and amazing atmosphere, do not hesitate to drop us a line. We are always ready to serve the best meal for every family around.

What We Serve

Hot Fresh Pizza, Pasta Dishes, Subs

There is a long long list of the meals we serve at Luigi's. From salads, appetizers and side orders, to entrees like chicken, shripms and salmon, we take care that our costumers are always happy with their orders.

Our menu also contains several dishes from a long range of italian pastas (spaghetti, linguine, ravioli, lasagna, tortellini etc.), pizzas, and italian subs (meatballs, calzone, chicken parmigiana). We also serve meals for kids in small portions. Lastly, our catering service takes care of hungry costumers anyhere in Denton , by providing fast delivery and the best prices.
